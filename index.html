our security philosophy (important)

andrew trusts anthropic's "public benefit corporation" status, but he understands that most people feel uneasy about ai security in general

the best results for building with claude's assistance come from working in "yolo" mode, where it has the "god" privileges to consider and do more things on our behalf

this comes with the risk that claude could deliberately or inadvertently damage our junk

this seems like a conundrum but actually there's a way to achieve both: just keep the personal junk totally separate from the "building" junk. this is actually pretty simple to achieve if we:

  • have a separate login identity from our personal account - we STRONGLY recommend registering a new, totally separate gmail address for this
  • create a separate low-privilege account on your machine, so claude can't see any of your personal junk in your normal account
  • install all our software locally to the low-privilege user so that it has zero impact and you can delete it in a few seconds, no impact
